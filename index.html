About Me

Currently located in Austin, Texas, I have been bartending for over 10 years. During the bizarre years of the global pandemic (2020-2022) I wanted to restructure my life, my goals, and my career. I came across a free SheCodes webinar to see if coding would be something that peaked my interest. Lo and behold, 15 weeks later, I have sparked a new and exciting fascination for coding where I have been able to pull from my creative and analytical brain to learn a new language and produce art through a computer screen.
